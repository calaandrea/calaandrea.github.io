Andrea Calà

Andrea Calà is an Italian photographer based in Chicago, Illinois, working primarily between the United States and Europe.

His practice engages with residual images, fragments, and states of suspension, often developing over extended periods and not always within fixed project structures. Through long-term accumulation and editorial re-reading, photography becomes a reflective and experiential practice — a way to trace continuity, ambiguity, and the shifting relationship between lived experience and meaning.
